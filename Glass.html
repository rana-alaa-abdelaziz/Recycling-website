Glass waste recycling :

is the process of reusing and repurposing discarded or unwanted glass products .

Recycling glass also conserves natural resources, including raw material, energy, and water, as it requires less energy

to produce glass from recycled materials than it does from raw material.

Do You Want To Know More About The Process?

what is flat glass?

Flat glass waste refers to any glass that is no longer usable or needed, including glass from windows, mirrors, and other flat glass products. This type of waste can come from residential, commercial, or industrial sources, and it can create environmental and health risks if not properly disposed of or recycled

what is container glass?

Container glass waste refers to any glass packaging material that is used for storing or transporting goods, such as bottles and jars, and is no longer reusable or recycl able. This type of glass waste is commonly found in households and businesses, and if not disposed of properly can contribute to environmental problems like pollution and landfill waste

Metal waste recycling :

is the process of recovering and converting scrap metal into usable products or raw materials.

One of the main benefits is the reduction of environmental impact. Recycling metals decreases the amount of metal waste that ends up in landfills, contaminate surrounding soil and water. The recycling of metals also saves energy,as it requires less energy to recycle metal than to extract it from raw materials. Recycling metals also conserves natural resources

What is Metal Waste?

Hazard metals are metals that can pose a risk to human health or the environment when they are not properly disposed of. These metals include lead, mercury, cad mium, arsen ic, and chromium, among others. Exposure to these metals can cause a range of health problems, including respiratory issues, neurological damage, and cancer

How we will deal with Metal Waste?

We will filter these materials into groups contains same type then give each group to competent authorities that will re-use it in their manufacture which will reduce the amount of energy consumed by manufacturing raw materials.
